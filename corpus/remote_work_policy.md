# Remote Work Policy

## Overview
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval.

## Eligibility
- All full-time employees are eligible for remote work.

## Expectations
- Maintain regular work hours.
- Be available for virtual meetings.
- Ensure a productive work environment.

## Security
- Use company-approved devices and VPN.

## References
- For questions, contact IT or HR.
