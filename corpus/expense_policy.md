# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Overview
Employees will be reimbursed for reasonable business expenses incurred during work.

## Eligible Expenses
- Travel (airfare, lodging, meals)
- Office supplies
- Client entertainment (with approval)

## Submission
- Submit expense reports within 30 days of the expense.
- Include itemized receipts.

## Approval
- Manager approval required for all reimbursements.

## References
- For questions, contact Finance.
