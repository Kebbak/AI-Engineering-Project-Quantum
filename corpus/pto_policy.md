# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

## Overview
Our company provides paid time off (PTO) to support work-life balance and employee well-being.

## Accrual
- Employees accrue PTO at a rate of 1.5 days per month.
- PTO can be used for vacation, illness, or personal matters.

## Requesting PTO
- Submit PTO requests at least 2 weeks in advance.
- PTO is subject to manager approval.

## Carryover
- Up to 5 unused PTO days may be carried over to the next year.
